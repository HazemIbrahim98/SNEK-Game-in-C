


							Game Rules


				1) You Can Change The Console Size In The Main Menu which
					Changes Broder (Game Looks Best Without Change).

				2) You Control The Snake Movement With W-A-S-D.

				3) Hitting The Border Or Your Tail Causes The Game To End.

				4) Eating The Red Apple Causes The Snake To Get Taller.

				5) In Hard Endless Snake Gets Smaller Over Time

				6) In Borderless Endless Half Of Rule #3 Dosen't Apply

						Press Any Key To Continue...
